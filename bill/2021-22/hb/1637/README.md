# HB 1637 - Mental illness/sentencing
*Status: First reading, referred to Public Safety (Not Officially read and referred until adoption of Introduction report).* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=1637&Year=2021)

Allowing a court to mitigate a criminal sentence when the defendant was experiencing mental illness at the time of the offense.
