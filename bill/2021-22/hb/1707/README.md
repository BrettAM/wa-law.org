# HB 1707 - Flotation device use
*Status: First reading, referred to Community & Economic Development.* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=1707&Year=2021)

Requiring the wearing of personal flotation devices on kayaks, canoes, and stand-up paddleboards.
