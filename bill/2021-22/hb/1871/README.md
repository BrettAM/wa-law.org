# HB 1871 - Establishing a moratorium on the siting of alternative energy facilities through the energy facility site evaluation council process pending a comprehensive performance report on the effects of the energy independence act and the recommendations of a joint legislative committee.
*Status: Prefiled for introduction.* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=1871&Year=2021)

Establishing a moratorium on the siting of alternative energy facilities through the energy facility site evaluation council process pending a comprehensive performance report on the effects of the energy independence act and the recommendations of a joint legislative committee.
