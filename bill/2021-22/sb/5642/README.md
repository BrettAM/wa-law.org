# SB 5642 - Affordable housing/REET
**Primary Sponsor:** [Mark Mullet](/person/leg/mark.mullet.md)

*Status: First reading, referred to Housing & Local Government.* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=5642&Year=2021)

Exempting a sale or transfer of real property for affordable housing to a nonprofit entity, housing authority, public corporation, county, or municipal corporation from the real estate excise tax.
