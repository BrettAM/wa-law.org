# SB 5838 - Diaper subsidy/TANF
*Status: First reading, referred to Human Services, Reentry & Rehabilitation.* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=5838&Year=2021)

Providing a monthly diaper subsidy for parents or other caregivers receiving temporary assistance for needy families.
