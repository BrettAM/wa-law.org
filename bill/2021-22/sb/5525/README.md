# SB 5525 - Water quality actions
*Status: First reading, referred to Environment, Energy & Technology (Not Officially read and referred until adoption of Introduction report).* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=5525&Year=2021)

Allowing persons to take immediate action to remedy water quality conditions to protect public health and welfare without the threat of penalty.
