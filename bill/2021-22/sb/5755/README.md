# SB 5755 - Vacant land redevelopment
*Status: First reading, referred to Housing & Local Government.* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=5755&Year=2021)

Authorizing certain cities to establish a limited sales and use tax incentive program to encourage redevelopment of vacant lands in urban areas.
